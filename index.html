Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 29, 2025

Campus TeamUp (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: Name, email, and profile details when you register.

Content Data: Posts, chats, vacancies, project details, and any content you share.

Device Data: Basic device info (Android version, device ID) for app performance and analytics.

Usage Data: How you interact with the app (searches, filters, features used).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve app features (team matching, chat, projects).

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

4. User-Generated Content

You are responsible for the content you post. Any inappropriate, offensive, or harmful content may result in removal or account suspension.

5. Security

We use encryption and secure databases (Firebase) to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

  • You can delete your posts or account at any time.
  • You can request correction or removal of your data by contacting us at the email below.

7. Children’s Privacy

Campus TeamUp is intended for college students (13+). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
